Selected Works, 2023–2026

Oil on linen and canvas

"I keep returning to the same room and finding it completely changed."

Mara Solis works in oil on linen and canvas. Her paintings return again and again to domestic interiors — rooms in the particular quality of light that precedes a storm, or follows one. She is based in Lisbon and shows regularly across Europe and North America.
